I’m passionate about discovering customer needs and delivering UX solutions that address those needs, while creating value for the business. I have the soft skills to collaborate with engineers, product and other designers. I am capable of conducting UX research, conducting usability testing, and making quick sketches to articulate design decisions on the fly. I am humble, but I have a strong personality and the communication skills to help drive decisions and create alignment within a team.
